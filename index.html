Effective Date: June 2026

At Word Puzzle Crossword, developed by Pevah Tech Solution (a division of Pevah Computer Services), we are committed to maintaining the trust of our players. This policy explains how we handle data and transaction security in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

1. No Logins & Anonymous Gameplay

We value your privacy. Word Puzzle Crossword does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal identifiers like your name or email address to enjoy the game. Gameplay progresses completely anonymously.

2. Cloud Data Storage & Game State

To deliver a consistent gameplay experience, save puzzle progression, and sync dynamic configurations, our application utilizes Google Firebase Realtime Database.

  • Data is saved using non-personally identifiable, system-generated continuous device tokens or unique installation IDs.
  • No email addresses, structural profiles, or contact credentials are processed or stored within our database servers.

3. In-App Purchases & Payments

We offer premium digital items and features through in-app purchases. All monetary transactions are processed securely by the native app distribution store platforms (Google Play Commerce or Apple App Store Pricing). We do not collect, process, or store credit card details, mobile money pins, or financial accounts on our servers.

4. Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

To keep our base application accessible, we display in-game advertisements. These network platforms may process technical hardware strings (such as Google Advertising ID or IDFA) to regulate capping and display relevant ads. Our infrastructure uses:

  • Google AdMob: For serving tailored advertising and promotional placements.
  • Firebase Database: For storing app scores, settings, and progression tokens anonymously.
